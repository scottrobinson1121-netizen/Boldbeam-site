FAQ

Questions agents ask.

Will my leads know it's an AI?

Not unless you tell them. LeadPilot is trained to sound like a human assistant — it uses your name and brokerage, respects your voice, and doesn't give itself away. We can even brand it as "Jordan's Assistant" rather than a generic name. In compliance-strict states (CA, NY, FL), we can add a one-time disclosure.

How is this different from Follow Up Boss or kvCORE?

Those are CRMs with workflow automation — they require you to write every follow-up yourself. LeadPilot is an AI that actually converses with your lead in real time, asks qualifying questions, books showings, and only hands off when the lead is ready. Think of LeadPilot as the layer that sits ON TOP of your existing CRM — it syncs both ways with Follow Up Boss and kvCORE.

What if the AI says something wrong?

For your first 5 conversations, LeadPilot drafts the reply and waits for your one-tap approval. After that, you can let it fly or keep the approval step on high-value conversations (sellers, luxury, relocation). You can jump in any conversation at any time. There's a compliance layer that blocks any discriminatory language or fair-housing violations.

How long does it take to set up?

About 10 minutes. Forward one Zillow lead email so we can set up the parser, connect your Google Calendar, upload 3–5 writing samples, and you're live. Most agents are responding to leads within 15 minutes of signup.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Month-to-month, cancel in one click. If LeadPilot doesn't book you an appointment in your first 30 days, we refund you. We don't want your money if it's not working.

Do I need a separate phone number?

No. LeadPilot gets you a local Twilio number that matches your area code, pre-registered for A2P 10DLC compliance, so your texts actually deliver. Your leads can text that number or your existing number — both flow into LeadPilot.
